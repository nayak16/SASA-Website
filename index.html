Who are we?

We are proud to present to you the Mayur South Asian Student Alliance, or MayurSASA for short, of Carnegie Mellon University. MayurSASA is the premier South Asian student organization on campus. It is also one of the biggest organizations on campus, with over 300 members.

Our Goal

Our main goal is to unite the South Asian community of Carnegie Mellon University and allow students to take part in events they want to get involved in

What we do

Each year MayurSASA holds a varity of events both on and off campus. MayurSASA is usually involved with around four or five large events: Bhangra in the Burgh, Mayur Show, Booth, and Diwali/Eid Celebration. In addition to the larger events, we have many smaller events throughout the year.
